Introduction
------------

iancoleman.io/bip39/ results are shown below a line for comparison and notated (IC).

bip32jp.github.io/english/ results are shown below a line for comparison and notated (JP).

Trezor Safe 3 hardware wallet results are shown below a line for comparison and notated (Trezor).


iancoleman.io/bip39/ does not support testnet so cannot use this to verify testnet functionality.

bip32jp.github.io/english/ has some support for testnet.

bip32jp.github.io/english/ is recommended on iancoleman.io/bip39/.


Testing not complete, but a reasonable initial coverage is provided.


Child index limits :
2^32 = 4294967296
2^32 - 1 = 4294967295
2^31 = 2147483648
2^31 - 1 = 2147483647
